# Switchboard Modules

## Billing360

Billing360 handles invoice creation, customer selection, line items, draft/saved invoice state, print/PDF flows, and payment entry points.

Key demonstrated capabilities:

- invoice creation
- customer / company attachment
- PayMe checkout surface
- Sui USDC payment option
- Stripe payment option
- transaction ID capture
- paid-state update

## Storage360

Storage360 is the backup, storage, and receipt layer.

Key demonstrated capabilities:

- backup controls
- backup history
- wallet-aware records
- receipt display
- Walrus-oriented storage flow
- Sui transaction visibility

## Treasury360

Treasury360 is the financial visibility layer.

Key demonstrated capabilities:

- connected wallet state
- Sui balance visibility
- treasury overview
- payment context
- future yield/position surface

## Documents360

Documents360 is the document workflow and document record module. It is intended to support business documents, files, attestations, and document intelligence.

## Automation360

Automation360 is the workflow layer for operational triggers, status changes, and workflow execution.

## Comms360

Comms360 supports messages, conversations, communication records, and related operational surfaces.

## AI Sidecars

AI sidecars provide in-context guidance at the module level. The goal is to keep AI assistance attached to work instead of isolating it in a separate chat tool.
