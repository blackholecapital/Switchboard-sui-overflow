# Three Minute Demo Script

## 0:00 - 0:15 Opening

"This is Switchboard OS, a modular business operating system where normal business workflows can use Sui payments, attestations, storage records, and AI assistance in the background."

## 0:15 - 0:45 Create Invoice

Open Billing360. Select a customer. Create a simple invoice. Show customer defaults and invoice form.

## 0:45 - 1:15 Payment Surface

Open the payment surface. Show Sui USDC and Stripe options. Explain that traditional and blockchain-native payments can share the same business workflow.

## 1:15 - 1:45 Sui Receipt / CRM Update

Show the transaction ID, paid-state update, receipt, and related CRM/pipeline status.

## 1:45 - 2:15 Storage360

Open Storage360. Show backup controls, receipt records, wallet signer, and storage history.

## 2:15 - 2:40 Treasury360

Open Treasury360. Show connected wallet state, balance visibility, and payment context.

## 2:40 - 3:00 Close

"The point is simple: Web3 should support business operations in the background. Switchboard brings payments, receipts, storage, treasury, CRM, and AI workflows into one operating system."
